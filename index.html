gryphon tea company story

Our founder Tian Wee has had a long love affair with tea. As a teenager, he first joined the family business as a volunteer apprentice, taking the time to learn the basics of trade. As his first job, he helped in all aspects to get himself acquainted with the product first-hand.

Through Gryphon Tea, Tian Wee expresses his creativity by creating exciting and innovative tea blends. His inspiration comes from interacting with people, places and food. Gaining exposure to fine food and wine has also helped him develop a unique style of tea blending. Rather than creating mass market teas, Tian Wee constantly evolves tea blends to suit the palate of his clients who range among fine restaurants, cafes and hotels.
